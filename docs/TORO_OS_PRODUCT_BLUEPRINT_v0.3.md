# TORO OS Product Blueprint v0.3 SAFE

Source: Airtable base `TORO OS - Master Brain`, table `31 TORO OS Build Blueprint`.

Primary record: `TORO OS Autonomous Business Operator Prompt v0.3 SAFE`.

## Direction

TORO OS is the foundation for an AI business operator, not just a dashboard. Product name is TORO OS only. Do not use Santoro. Ignore old builds, old UI assumptions and hotel-only limitations.

Dreamcatcher is the first workspace/demo, but TORO OS must stay universal.

## Final Vision

TORO OS should understand the business deeply, connect to tools, research the market, prepare campaigns, prepare pricing recommendations, prepare channel content/photo updates, coordinate operations, create Codex tasks, review previews and queue business actions for approval.

Future execution must happen only through explicit permissions, connectors and approval gates.

## Core Principle

Business Knowledge + Market Research + Tool Access + Agent Reasoning + Permissions + Human Approval = Safe Business Execution.

## Authority Rules

- Kross is authority for live commercial data.
- Airtable/TORO OS is structured business/content brain.
- Dropbox is original media archive.
- Notion is memory/documentation, not live operational truth.
- GitHub is code authority.
- Vercel is deployment/preview authority.
- Alegra is accounting authority when connected.
- OpenAI powers reasoning.
- Codex builds code.

## Required Modules

Command Home, Business Brain, Tool Hub/Connector Center, Agent Command Center, Revenue & Pricing Control, Campaign Studio, Channel Content Control, Market Research Center, Kross Control Center, Communication Center, Operations Floor, Assets Curation Pipeline, Data Brain, Governance/Rules Engine, Tasks & Approvals, Browser Control, Codex Builder Queue, Settings/Workspaces/Roles.

## Safety

Do not perform direct external writes in v0.3. Build safe operational scaffolding with approvals, logs and queued actions.

Every screen/card must show source, status, risk, confidence, approval requirement and next action.
