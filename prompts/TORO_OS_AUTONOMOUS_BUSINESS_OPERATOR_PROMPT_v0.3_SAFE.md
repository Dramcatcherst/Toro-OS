# TORO OS Autonomous Business Operator Prompt v0.3 SAFE

Source: Airtable record `TORO OS Autonomous Business Operator Prompt v0.3 SAFE`.

Build TORO OS as the foundation for an AI business operator, not just a dashboard.

Before coding, study the full Airtable base `TORO OS - Master Brain`. If direction is unclear, always return to Airtable base `TORO OS - Master Brain`, table `31 TORO OS Build Blueprint`, and reread the latest Official v0.3/v0.2 records.

Product name: TORO OS. Never use Santoro. Ignore old builds, old UI assumptions and hotel-only limitations. Dreamcatcher is the first workspace/demo, but TORO OS must be universal.

Final vision: TORO OS should understand the business deeply, connect to tools, research the market, prepare campaigns, prepare pricing recommendations, prepare channel content/photo updates, coordinate operations, create Codex tasks, review previews and queue business actions for approval. Future execution must happen only through explicit permissions, connectors and approval gates.

Core principle: Business Knowledge + Market Research + Tool Access + Agent Reasoning + Permissions + Human Approval = Safe Business Execution.

Mandatory database study: model the Airtable tables Source Registry, Properties, Villas, Rooms, Sellable Units, Amenities, Experiences, Kross References, Assets Registry, Website Pages, Tasks, QA Conflicts, Governance Rules, Guest Communication, Guests/CRM Light, Reservations Snapshot, Operations, Staff & Providers, SOPs/Policies, Kross BE Content Master, Website V11 Conversion OS, V11 Master Control Center and TORO OS Build Blueprint.

Create TypeScript types and real-shaped mock data based on the Airtable structure. Prepare adapter/service scaffolds for future live integrations without unsafe writes in this build.

Action levels: observe, analyze, draft, recommend, prepare fields, queue task, execute low-risk action, execute with approval, blocked. Any sensitive or external-impacting action must be approval-gated.

Required modules: Command Home, Business Brain, Tool Hub/Connector Center, Agent Command Center, Revenue & Pricing Control, Campaign Studio, Channel Content Control, Market Research Center, Kross Control Center, Communication Center, Operations Floor, Assets Curation Pipeline, Data Brain, Governance/Rules Engine, Tasks & Approvals, Browser Control, Codex Builder Queue, Settings/Workspaces/Roles.

Every screen/card must show source, status, risk, confidence, approval requirement and next action.

Do not perform direct external writes in v0.3. Build safe operational scaffolding with approvals, logs and queued actions.
